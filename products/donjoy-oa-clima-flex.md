## description
```html
<p>Stay cool and comfortable while you offload knee osteoarthritis pain with the DonJoy OA Clima-Flex. It combines gentle compression with adjustable offloading to shift weight away from the worn side of the joint, all in a breathable, low-profile wraparound you can wear discreetly under clothing during everyday activity.</p>
<h3>Cooling C-6 Fabric Technology</h3>
<p>The Clima-Flex liner uses DonJoy's C-6 Fabric Technology - carbon threads woven into a moisture-wicking, highly breathable fabric that helps control temperature, naturally repels odour and is antimicrobial and soft against the skin. It's built for comfortable, cooling relief through the day.</p>
<h3>Adjustable Offloading</h3>
<p>DonJoy's 4-Points-of-Leverage™ system, with four degrees of correction built into the frame, shifts load from the painful compartment towards the healthier side of the joint. A keyless dual-action dial lets you fine-tune the amount of offloading on the fly, and semi-rigid cuffs concealed in the thigh and calf keep the support discreet. DonJoy reports the design is clinically shown to help keep the knee in a stable position.</p>
<h3>Easy to Apply, Easy to Adjust</h3>
<p>An E-Z Wraparound design with E-Z Grip handles makes the brace simple to put on for a form-fitting, comfortable result, and the thigh and calf straps adjust easily for more or less compression to suit you. Its ease of use has been recognised with the US Arthritis Foundation's Ease of Use commendation. Ideal for light activities such as walking, golf or hiking.</p>
<h3>Order Yours Today</h3>
<p>Cool, comfortable, adjustable relief for osteoarthritis knee pain - that's the DonJoy OA Clima-Flex. Order online today, or contact our team for help choosing the correct side and size.</p>
<hr/>
<h2>FAQs</h2>
<h3>What is the OA Clima-Flex used for?</h3>
<p>It is an offloading brace for mild-to-moderate one-sided (unicompartmental) osteoarthritis of the knee. It uses compression and adjustable offloading to move load away from the degenerative compartment and help relieve pain.</p>
<h3>Which side and size do I need?</h3>
<p>Choose the configuration that offloads your affected compartment (medial or lateral). For size, measure your thigh circumference 15 cm (6") above the middle of your kneecap and compare it with the size guide. Contact us if you're unsure.</p>
<h3>How do I adjust the offloading?</h3>
<p>Offloading is set at the hinge using the keyless dual-action dial, which can be adjusted on the fly without tools. The thigh and calf straps separately adjust the level of compression for comfort.</p>
<h3>Can I exercise while wearing it?</h3>
<p>Yes - it's designed for light activities such as walking, golf and hiking. Gentle exercise is generally beneficial for osteoarthritis; ease off if you feel discomfort and seek advice if pain persists.</p>
<h3>Does it get hot to wear?</h3>
<p>The Clima-Flex liner is specifically designed to stay cool: its breathable, moisture-wicking C-6 fabric helps control temperature for comfortable all-day wear.</p>
```
