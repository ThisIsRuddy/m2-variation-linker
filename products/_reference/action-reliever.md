## description
```html
<p>Ease discomfort and enhance mobility with the Action Reliever Knee Brace, a revolutionary solution designed to provide dynamic off-loading support for individuals dealing with medial or lateral osteoarthritis and meniscal tears. Crafted with precision and comfort in mind, this brace integrates innovative features to ensure ease of use, all-day comfort, and discreet wear under clothing.</p>
<h3>Dynamic Off-Loading System</h3>
<p>Experience targeted relief with our dynamic three-point pressure system, strategically engineered to be most effective during heel strike. The brace incorporates straps that create a dynamic pressure system, coupled with a combined rigid adjustable hinge on the affected side and a flexible stay on the unaffected side, ensuring optimal support precisely where it's needed. Plus, the front straps allow for self-adjustment, catering to individual comfort preferences.</p>
<h3>Ease of Use</h3>
<p>Say goodbye to cumbersome fittings with our magnetic buckles, facilitating a simple two-step fitting process. Putting on and taking off the brace is effortless, allowing you to set it up at home without any hassle.</p>
<h3>Comfort</h3>
<p>Designed for all-day wear, the Action Reliever boasts anatomically shaped straps that are self-oriented and adjustable for personalised comfort. The anatomical knitting ensures there are no compression areas at the popliteal crease or brace edges, guaranteeing continuous comfort throughout the day. Its slim and discreet profile means you can wear it under clothing without feeling bulky or conspicuous.</p>
<h3>Order Yours Today</h3>
<p>Take control of your knee health with the Action Reliever Knee Brace. Whether it's osteoarthritis or meniscal tears, our brace offers targeted relief and support, allowing you to move with confidence and comfort. Purchase directly from our website or consult with our team for personalised assistance.</p>
<hr/>
<h2>FAQs</h2>
<h3>How many hours per day should I wear my Action Reliever knee brace?</h3>
<p>We recommend gradually increasing wear time, starting with a few hours a day and gradually extending it over the first week.</p>
<h3>How often should I wear my Action Reliever?</h3>
<p>The frequency of wear depends on your comfort level and activity level. Wear it during active periods or when experiencing knee pain for optimal support.</p>
<h3>Can I do exercise in my Action Reliever knee brace?</h3>
<p>Yes, physical activity is beneficial while wearing the brace. Gradually incorporate strengthening exercises to improve mobility and reduce pain.</p>
<h3>Will wearing a knee brace make me weaker?</h3>
<p>No, wearing the Action Reliever knee brace can actually improve muscle power and stability in the affected leg.</p>
<h3>How do I wash the Action Reliever?</h3>
<p>It's recommended to hand wash or machine wash with cold water. Remove metal hinges before washing and allow to air dry.</p>
```
